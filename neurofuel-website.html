Frequently Asked Questions

Most customers report noticing cognitive benefits within the first week of consistent use, with optimal results typically achieved after 2-4 weeks of daily supplementation. Individual results may vary based on factors like diet, sleep, and stress levels.

NeuroFuel is formulated to be well-tolerated with minimal side effects. Some individuals may experience mild digestive discomfort when first starting, which typically subsides within a few days. As with any supplement, we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider before use.

For optimal results, we recommend taking NeuroFuel in the morning with breakfast. Avoid taking it late in the day as some ingredients may affect sleep if taken too close to bedtime. Our "Neuro Sleep" formula is specifically designed for evening use.

While NeuroFuel is generally safe to take with most supplements, we recommend spacing it out from other supplements by 1-2 hours for optimal absorption. If you're taking prescription medications, please consult with your healthcare provider before adding any new supplement to your regimen.
